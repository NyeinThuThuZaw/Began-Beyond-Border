Discover the beauty of Bagan

Events


"Events Hot Air Balloon Rides: Hot air balloon rides over Bagan are a popular activity, allowing tourists to witness the vastness of the archaeological site from the air, providing a unique and breathtaking perspective."



Festivals

Bagan celebrates various traditional festivals throughout the year, such as the Ananda Pagoda Festival and Tabaung Festival. These events involve religious ceremonies, processions, traditional music and dance performances, providing a glimpse into the cultural and spiritual heritage of the community.
